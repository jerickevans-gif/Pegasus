# Code of Conduct

Pegasus is a project for designers learning to use AI tools. Most contributors are not full-time engineers.

**Be helpful. Be specific. Assume good faith.**

That means:
- When someone asks a "basic" question, answer it directly. No "you should know this."
- When you spot a mistake in code, suggest the fix — don't just point.
- When you disagree, say *why* and offer an alternative.

**What's not OK:**
- Personal attacks, harassment, gatekeeping
- Mocking someone's level of dev experience
- Sexist/racist/homophobic/ableist language

If you see something that crosses a line, email <jerick@eigenhitchens.com>. Reports are confidential.

This applies in PRs, issues, discussions, and any other Pegasus-adjacent space.
