# CLAUDE.md — Scroll-narrative case study

A long-form scrollable case study (the kind you might link to from your portfolio).

## Structure

The file is broken into clearly-labeled sections. Each `<section>` is one beat in the story. Standard order:

1. Hero (title + meta)
2. Problem
3. Research
4. Approach
5. Decisions (one section per decision)
6. Result
7. Impact (with stat blocks)
8. What I'd do differently

Drop `<figure>` blocks between sections for hero shots, screens, or sketches.

## Working with me

- Paste the case study text in any rough form and ask Claude to slot it into the sections.
- Ask Claude to suggest where to add visuals.
- Ask Claude to write the "What I'd do differently" section based on the prior sections.

## Deploy

`pegasus deploy` ships it. Link to the URL from your portfolio's project grid.
