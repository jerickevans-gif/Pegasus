# CLAUDE.md — Link-in-bio page

Your own version of Linktree, hosted on your own domain.

## Working with me

- Add or remove links by editing the `<div class="mt-10 space-y-3">` block in `index.html`.
- Drop a headshot at `./avatar.jpg` and update the `<div class="w-24 h-24...">` to show it.
- Theme it: change `from-stone-50 to-stone-200` to any Tailwind color pair.

## Deploy

`pegasus deploy` ships it. Use the URL as your Instagram/Twitter bio link.
