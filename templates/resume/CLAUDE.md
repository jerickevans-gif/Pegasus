# CLAUDE.md — Resume

A printable single-page CV that doubles as a web page. Tailwind via CDN.

## How to use

- Edit `index.html` directly. Each section is clearly labeled.
- The `@media print` block tunes margins for US Letter — Cmd-P / Ctrl-P → Save as PDF.
- The "no-print" footer card disappears when printing.

## Working with me

- Paste your old resume text and ask Claude to slot it into this template.
- Ask Claude to re-tone bullets ("make these more outcome-focused").
- Ask Claude to suggest swaps for weak verbs.

## Deploy

`pegasus deploy` puts it online at a URL you can put on your physical resume.
