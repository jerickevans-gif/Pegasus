## Templates

All templates in `templates/` are written for Pegasus (MIT). Designers using them are welcome to remove the optional "Made with Pegasus" footer credit — it's never required.

## The "Made with Pegasus" badge

The `badge.svg` at the repo root is **completely optional**. Designers can use it on their portfolio sites if they want to share what built their site, or omit it entirely. Pegasus places no requirement to credit it anywhere on user-built work.

## How to be added

Open a PR! The fastest path:
1. Submit a useful skill or template
2. Use a permissive license (MIT preferred)
3. Add yourself to the relevant table above

## Reporting an attribution miss

If your work is in Pegasus and not credited, that's a bug — open an issue at <https://github.com/jerickevans-gif/Pegasus/issues> and I'll fix it within 24h.
