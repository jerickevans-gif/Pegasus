    Welcome to Pegasus

    Three things to know:

    1. 1
      Pick your track at the top: 🎨 UX Design or 💼 Job Search. Filters everything.
    2. 2
      Click any card to copy its command/prompt. Paste into your VS Code terminal or Claude/OpenCode.
    3. 3
      Search anything: press ⌘K for the palette, or / to filter inline.

    Steps — click any to copy

      💡 Tip: paste step-by-step to follow along, or use Copy all prompts to drop the full recipe into one chat.

      Keyboard shortcuts

      • / — focus the inline search
      • ⌘ + K — open the command palette
      • ? — show this help
      • Esc — close palette / clear search
      • ↑ ↓ — navigate palette results
      • Enter — copy / open the selected item
      • g + section letter — jump to section (g+t templates, g+c commands…)
