Welcome to Pegasus

Press ⌘K for the command palette, / to search, or ? for shortcuts. Pick your track above to filter.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • / — focus the inline search
  • ⌘ + K — open the command palette
  • ? — show this help
  • Esc — close palette / clear search
  • ↑ ↓ — navigate palette results
  • Enter — copy / open the selected item
  • g + section letter — jump to section (g+t templates, g+c commands…)
